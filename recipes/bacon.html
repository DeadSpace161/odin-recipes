Home Cured Brown Sugar and Pepper Bacon Recipe

There is a whole lot of truth to the saying that “the way to a man’s heart is thru his stomach”. However in our house the saying is “…thru some pork belly.”

    Ingredients

  • 3-4 lbs fresh pork belly
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp coarse black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp ground bay leaf
  • 1 tsp granulated onion
  • 1 tsp granulated garlic
  • 1/2 tsp ground thyme
  • 1 tsp pink curing salt #1

    Cooking Instructions

  1. In a plastic container or Zip-Loc bag, mix the salt, brown sugar, black pepper, bay leaf, garlic, onion, and thyme together well
  2. Once satisfied with flavor/smell, add the pink curing salt
  3. Place pork belly into marinade and refrigerate for 7-10 days; minimum of a week
  4. After 3-4 days in the cure, flip pork belly slabs to allow for more even curing
  5. Once cured, rinse pork belly with cold water to remove accumulated/extra salt
  6. If wanting smoked bacon, smoke pork belly at 200F-215F until pork belly reaches 155F
  7. Slice bacon to desired thickness; if smoked, bacon is ready to eat. Otherwise, pan fry or bake!
